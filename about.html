About Us

At Park Finder, we share your love for the outdoors. We are working hard to bring you more parks and an easier way to plan for your next trip. Thanks to Google, Open Weather and the National Park Service for being a part of this project, we could not have done it without you. Please feel free to leave us a comment, a question or anything you'd like, we would love to know how your trip went.

Thank you for coming, we hope you found a great park to visit!

-The Park Finder Team
